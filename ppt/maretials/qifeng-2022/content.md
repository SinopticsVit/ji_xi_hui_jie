QIFENG PRECISION INDUSTRY SCI-TECH CORP. is a high-tech enterprise which is specialized in R&D, production,
sales and service of medium and high-end fasteners and listed on the Beijing Stock Exchange (stock code: 873169). QIFENG
was awarded the Jiaxing Mayor Quality Award in 2020, the "Specialized and New" small and Medium enterprises in Zhejiang
Province and "The Hidden Champions" enterprises in 2021, and the fourth batch of "Specialized and New" "Little Giants"
enterprises by the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology in 2022.

The company specializes in the development and production of high-strength, high-precision, high-reliability aerospace,
rail transit, civil and industrial construction, wind power and photovoltaic new energy fasteners, etc. The product covers all
kinds of standard fasteners and special-shaped customized parts, and the product material includes carbon steel, alloy steel,
stainless steel,high temperature alloy and titanium alloy, etc, The company has the ability to provide customers with the whole
process service of R&D, design, production and testing of medium and high-end fasteners, and has benn successfully selected
as a qualified supplier of CNNC, CRRC, VOSSLOH and other well-known company at home and abroad.
