## Company Overview

Jixi Huijie Aircraft Research and Development Center Co., Ltd. is a technology-driven enterprise based in Jixi City, Heilongjiang Province, operating across the general aviation and aerospace manufacturing sectors. Since its establishment in August 2021, the company has focused on aircraft R&D, technology transfer, and industrialization, building a comprehensive business portfolio spanning agricultural and forestry aviation operations, police and special-purpose helicopter development, aviation spare parts supply, and international trade.

Leveraging its existing aviation operations, the company is actively pursuing a strategic fleet upgrade from fixed-wing to helicopter platforms and, together with partners, implementing light aircraft and helicopter production and sales projects. The long-term goal is to build an integrated general aviation ecosystem encompassing operations, services, and manufacturing.

## Strategic Upgrade: Helicopters Replacing Fixed-Wing Aircraft

The company is implementing a **"Helicopters Replacing Fixed-Wing Aircraft"** strategy to improve operational flexibility and overall efficiency.

### Strategic Framework

1. **Holding Structure**: A 60% controlling stake structure retaining management control while opening 40% equity to strategic investors.
2. **Financing-Driven Upgrade**: Seeking **RMB 10–20 million** in financing specifically for helicopter acquisition and supporting equipment to drive fleet modernization.
3. **Strategic Synergy**: Accelerating the comprehensive replacement of fixed-wing aircraft with helicopters through external capital and resource injection.
4. **Industrial Ecosystem**: Using capital as a link to build a closed-loop general aviation industry integrating operations, services, and manufacturing.

### Aviation Service Upgrade Plan

| Area | Initiatives |
|---|---|
| Use of Funds | Dedicated helicopter acquisition; maintenance system improvement for long-term operational efficiency |
| Capability Enhancement | Complex-terrain operations (mountains, forests); improved response times; shorter mission preparation and execution cycles |
| Business Expansion | Expanded agricultural spraying; forest patrol and ecological resource monitoring |
| Market Strategy | Regional first-mover advantage; localized service model; brand building |
| Technology Introduction | High-performance helicopters; advanced avionics integration for precision complex missions |

### Key Advantages of Helicopters over Fixed-Wing Aircraft

- **Hovering Capability**: Adaptation to mountains, forests, and other complex terrain with precise position control;
- **Low-Speed Flight**: Stable flight at low speeds for fine spraying; reduced chemical drift and missed spots;
- **Precision Application**: On-demand quantitative spraying integrated with navigation and application systems;
- **Flexible Takeoff & Landing**: No runway required; vertical operations in small field areas; reduced transition time;
- **Rapid Response**: Quick deployment and transfer for emergency prevention and pest outbreak control;
- **Reduced Infrastructure Dependency**: No dependence on airports or fixed facilities; lower infrastructure investment;
- **Cost Efficiency**: High utilization rates dilute unit operational costs with significant long-term economic advantages;
- **Improved Efficiency**: Approximately **30% higher** per-sortie efficiency compared to fixed-wing aircraft; all-weather high-efficiency operations.

### Operational Flexibility

Helicopters do not require dedicated airports and can operate deep in fields and forest areas, significantly reducing operating costs and response times through flexible takeoff and landing, direct access to operation zones, improved coverage accuracy, reduced scheduling costs, and support for high-frequency, normalized aviation tasks.

## Police Helicopter Localization Project

The company is advancing the **introduction of Russian Ka-700 armed helicopter technology and localized police helicopter manufacturing**, filling the domestic gap in independent police helicopter R&D.

### Eight-Step Development Roadmap

1. **Introduce Core Technology** — Ka-700 core technology as the foundation for localization;
2. **Promote Domestic R&D** — Key system localization and enhanced independent R&D capabilities;
3. **Optimize Model Design** — "Type 700" helicopter tailored to domestic police requirements;
4. **Enhance Policing Capabilities** — Improved patrol, surveillance, and emergency response efficiency;
5. **Break Technical Bottlenecks** — Independent control of power systems, avionics integration, and other key technologies;
6. **Fill R&D Gaps** — Pioneer domestic police helicopter independent R&D with a complete technical system;
7. **Promote Equipment Deployment** — Equipment rollout to public security, forest firefighting, and related units;
8. **Seize the High-End Market** — Benchmark products for domestic police aviation; expanded market share in special aviation equipment.
