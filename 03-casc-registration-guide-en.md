# CASC Registration Guide (for Mainland China Companies)

This guide explains how a **Mainland China legal entity** can register on the CASC Aviation Materials platform:

- Registration page: [https://aero.cascpooling.com/new-module/register](https://aero.cascpooling.com/new-module/register)
- Platform home: [https://aero.cascpooling.com/](https://aero.cascpooling.com/)

---

## 1) Who can register

The registration form is intended for organizations in the aviation supply chain (airlines, MROs, and related companies), with company verification based on:

- **Unified Social Credit Code (USCC / 统一社会信用代码)** - 18 characters
- **Business License (营业执照)** upload

In practice, this means the applicant should be a **Mainland China registered entity** with a valid PRC business license.

---

## 2) Prepare before starting

Have the following ready:

1. **China mainland mobile phone number** (to receive SMS verification code)
2. **Email address** for account contact
3. **Contact person name** (at least 2 characters)
4. **Company name** (exact legal name, at least 2 characters)
5. **Registration Type** (see section 4 below)
6. **USCC** (18-character Unified Social Credit Code, exactly as on license)
7. **Business License images** (at least 1, max 2 files, each < 5 MB)
8. Optional: **Other qualification documents** (images only, up to 10 files, each < 5 MB)

---

## 3) Step-by-step registration

1. Open: [https://aero.cascpooling.com/new-module/register](https://aero.cascpooling.com/new-module/register)
2. Enter your **Phone Number**.
3. Request and enter the **6-digit Verification Code** (SMS).
4. Set **Password** and **Confirm Password**.
5. Enter **Email**.
6. Enter **Contact Name**.
7. Enter **Company Name** (must match your legal entity information).
8. Select **Registration Type**.
9. Enter **Unified Social Credit Code** (18 characters).
10. Upload **Business License** image file(s).
11. Upload **Other Qualification Documents** (if available/relevant).
12. Submit the registration form and wait for platform review/activation if required.

---

## 4) How to choose "Registration Type"

Use the category that matches your organization:

- **Airline (航司)**: Air carrier / airline operator.
- **Airline MRO (航司MRO)**: MRO unit under an airline group.
- **Other MRO (其他MRO)**: Independent third-party maintenance/repair organization.
- **Other (其他)**: Other aviation supply-chain participants (e.g., parts trader, service provider) that do not fit the first three categories.

If your business model can fit more than one category, choose the one that best reflects your **licensed core business** and supporting qualification documents.

---

## 5) Common validation issues

- **USCC format error**: must be exactly 18 alphanumeric characters (no spaces).
- **Company mismatch**: company name, USCC, and license details must be consistent.
- **File upload error**: upload image files only; respect file count and size limits.
- **SMS code timeout**: request a new code and re-enter within validity window.

---

## 6) Important note for Hong Kong / overseas entities

A Hong Kong company usually does **not** have a Mainland China USCC, so direct registration may fail on mandatory fields.

Typical solutions:

- Register with a **Mainland China legal entity** (subsidiary/affiliate) that has a valid USCC and PRC business license.
- Contact CASC support to ask whether an overseas entity workflow is available.
