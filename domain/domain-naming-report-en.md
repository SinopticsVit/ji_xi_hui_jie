**Date:** 14 June 2026

## Executive Summary

For international markets, the legal name **Jixi Huijie Aircraft Research and Development Center** is too long and hard to pronounce. The city **Jixi** has no recognition abroad; **Huijie** is phonetically difficult and risks confusion with other Chinese aviation firms (e.g. Chongqing **Huidi** Aviation).

| Role | Name | Domain |
|------|------|--------|
| Legal entity (unchanged) | Jixi Huijie Aircraft Research and Development Center Co., Ltd. | — |
| International trade name | **Pengbo Aviation** | pengboaviation.com |
| Chinese trade name | 鹏博航空 / 鸡西鹏博通航 | pengboaviation.com |
| Logo / mark | PBT / PENGBOTONGHANG | pengbo.aero |

**Primary recommendation:** **Pengbo Aviation** at **www.pengboaviation.com**, aligned with the existing logo brand in `assets/logo-pengbotonghang.svg`.

**Tagline:** *General Aviation. Components. Innovation.*

---

## Why Pengbo Aviation

1. **Existing brand asset** — Logo already uses 鸡西鹏博通航 / PENGBOTONGHANG.
2. **Pronounceable** — PENG-boh Av-ee-AY-shun works in English, Russian, and European languages.
3. **Industry clarity** — *Aviation* signals GA, MRO, components, and helicopter programs without narrowing to one product.
4. **Domain availability** — `pengboaviation.com` and `pengbo.aero` showed no DNS records on 14 Jun 2026 (confirm via WHOIS before purchase). `pengbo.com` is taken (parked since 2006).
